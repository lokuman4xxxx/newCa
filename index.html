Introduction

What is the "Price Match Policy"?

Many stores within North America use price matching as a practice, wherein a retailer agrees to sell a product for a lower price in order to price match the pricing of their competitors. However, though many retailers have this "lowest price guarantee", they cannot always keep track of the pricing of all the products from other retailers. Kiwinano is a price comparison and price match service site that works based on the Price Match Policy in Canada. We offer our users the ability to view, compare, and price match information gathered from some of the largest electronic product retailers. It is through Kiwinano that users can instantly find and price match any type of electronic product, within over 50 categories, including Consoles, Computers, Televisions, Cameras, and Home Appliances. It is through the use of Kiwinano that you can find the right product at the lowest price.

We at Kiwinano are here to offer you the best and the newest price match information available. You can browse all available price matches for a product, as listed by all electronic retailers, making us a one-stop overview for your price matching needs. We take it upon ourselves to find the best price matches out there for you, saving you not only money, but also the time it takes to price match on your own. By putting all the information into one simple and easy-to-use location, we make shopping comfortable and fun for everyone. Print out our "Price Match Receipt" and show it to the retailer of your choosing in order for you to get the product with its lowest price match right away.
